FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Let’s do our best to answer your most frequently asked questions.

How many agents can we add?

There are no limits to the number of agents you can add to a property. martur.io is an agent-centric chat application, which means every agent has their own account, and you can share properties.

Are there any Ads?

No, martur.io is completely ad-free. We focus on providing a seamless experience for both agents and customers.

How can you offer this for free?

We believe in empowering businesses with the best tools without financial barriers. Our free plan is supported by our premium features for those who need advanced capabilities.

Do you limit the number of concurrent chats?

No, there are no limits on the number of concurrent chats. You can handle as many conversations as needed.

Will I ever be charged to use the software?

Our basic features are free forever. However, we offer premium plans with additional features for businesses that need more advanced tools.

Where can I find documentation?

You can find detailed documentation in our Help Center.

Do you offer a self-hosted solution?

Currently, we do not offer a self-hosted solution. All our services are cloud-based for ease of use and scalability.
